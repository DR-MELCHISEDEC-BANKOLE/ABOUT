About Chris Ebute

Meet Your Instructor and Tutor

Hi, my name is Chris Ebute, hailing from Nigeria. I am a devout Christian with a passion for teaching and performing gospel music. Here's a brief overview of my background and teaching philosophy:

Music Education:

I have undergone training in various music schools in my local area and have also enriched my skills through online classes taught by reputable piano instructors.

Teaching Mission

My primary goal is to simplify the learning process for aspiring pianists interested in gospel music. I specialize in guiding students from absolute beginner levels to advanced proficiency.

Systematic Approach

My courses are structured in a systematic manner, ensuring gradual progress in your piano journey. Each lesson is carefully crafted to facilitate your growth until you achieve your aspirations in gospel piano.

Join me on this enriching journey, and together, we'll unlock the joy of playing gospel music on the piano.
